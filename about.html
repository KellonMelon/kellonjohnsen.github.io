Who am I?

  • Friends University - Presidential Honor Roll (4.0 GPA)
  • Eagle Scout
  • Principal Chair - Trumpet - KMEA State Honor Band 1234A

I am very active in multiple music ensembles, both in and out of school, some including jazz bands, concert wind bands and small jazz groups. I also sing in a church choir.
